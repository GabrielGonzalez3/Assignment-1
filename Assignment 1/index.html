Background

Sonic the hedgehog is abnormal hedgehog who is fast as the wind. In the games, comics, he protects his friends and stops the evil Doctor Eggman. He has been on many adventures through the depths of space, the books of the of the old, and his lastest one being the Starfall Islands. He has proven to have feats that make him the fastest thing alive. In the Archie Comics, it has been shown that Sonic can surpass light speed as he ran head first into a blackhole to save his home. In the more recent game, Sonic Froniters, Sonic is able to tap into chaos energy to create his moveset. Additionally, he is able to tap into the chaos energy to control time and space around him, but this feat requires a chaos emerald to use this ability. He has been known to fight deities such as Perfect Chaos, Solaris, Iblis, and more. He is someone that wouldn't just stand on the sidelines while his friends are fight, he is going to do everything he can to help them and protect them.

The Many Forms of Sonic

    Super Sonic

    • Super Sonic is one of the most classic transformations that Sonic has. Sonic must gather the seven Chaos Emeralds in order for him to transform. He use the positive energy of the Chaos Emeralds. Once transformed nothing can stop him. He gains the feats of super strength, hyper speed, flight, invulnerability and perfect chaos abilities. The chaos abilities include Chaos Snap, Chaos Spear, and Chaos Control. All of these would have increased range and potency. He now has red eyes and his fur now this golden color. However, this transformation requires rings to maintain, so if Sonic runs out of rings then goes back to his base form.

    Dark Sonic

    • Dark Sonic is a transformation that has only ever appeared once and that was in the show Sonic X. The same concept is applied with Dark Sonic similar to Super Sonic except Sonic use the negative energy of the Chaos Emeralds to transform. Once he transforms nothing can stop him from unleashing his wrath. He gains the same exact feats of Super Sonic however is blinded by rage and can't really control his actions. He no longer has pupils and all you see is a black hedgehog with glowing white where his should be. The same ring limitation still applies here so if he runs out then he returns back to base form.

    Darkspine Sonic

    • Darkspine Sonic is a transformation that was shown in the game Sonic and the Secret Rings. In the game, instead of the Chaos Emeralds there are the Seven World Rings. The main villian abosrbs the Seven World Rings Prayers, Sadness, Rage, Hatred, Joy, Pleasure, and Wishes are abosrbed by the Erazor Djinn and becomes Alf Layla wa-Layla. The rings are then sent out and Sonic absorbs the three of the World Rings. Rage, Sadness and Hatred are absorbed and Sonic then becomes Darkspine Sonic. Darkspine Sonic has the feats of increased speed, strength, flight, and the ability to slow and speed time. This is known as Time Break and Speed Break. He does not need rings to maintain this transformation and can release this transformation anytime that he wants to. Similar to Dark Sonic, Darkspine Sonic loses his pupils and now has a glowing white, his fur a combination of purple and white, he now has rings on his cuffs and ankles, his once red and white shoes are now in a purple and white color, and he is now surrounded in this flaming aura.

    Excalibur Sonic

    • Excalibur Sonic is a transformation that was shown in the game Sonic and the Black Knight. In the game, instead of the Chaos Emeralds there is the Sacred light. The Knights of the Round Table Sir Lancelot, Sir Gawain, and Sir Percival throw in their swords into the Sacred light and Sonic is engulfed in the light transforming him to Excalibur Sonic. Excalibur Sonic wields the sacred sword Excalibur as his weapon and is cover in this golden plated armor with a red cape flowing behind him. Excalibur Sonic has the feats of flight and swordsmanship. Similar to Darkspine, Exaclibur Sonic can release his form whenever he chooses to.

    Sonic the Werehog

    • The Werehog is a transformation that was shown in the game Sonic Unleashed. In the game, Sonic is infused with Dark Gaia energy transforms into the Werehog. The Werehog is kind of like a werewolf as the Werehog has razor sharp claws and fangs, grows a large coat of fur, and his ears are now perked up. Instead of his regular shoes there are now spikes coming from the bottom and surround the white part of shoe. Now instead of being fast like normal, he instead now has a major increase to his strength and his arms are now able to stretch to a certain range. Sonic can't control his transformation to the Werehog as this transformation is part of the day and night cycle. In the day there is Sonic the Hedgehog and at night there Sonic the Werehog.

    Super Sonic 2

    • Super Sonic 2 is the upgraded form to Super Sonic as this was an upgrade recieved from Master King after you completed his trial. Once you finished his trial, he gives Sonic the power to control his Cyber Corruption and now is able to use it as power. This power is only half of what Sonic can do with this power as the power never intended to bond with someone who isn't a Koco. Super Sonic 2 has upgraded feats to Super Sonic as he can do everything that Super Sonic can do but even better. Super Sonic 2 now has blue eyes instead of the normal red and is surrounded with this aura of gold and cyber corruption. Similar to Super Sonic, this transformation requires rings to maintain so once out of ring then Sonic returns to base form.

    Cyber Sonic

    • Cyber Sonic is the full power of the upgrade recieved from Master King. Once Sonic is in his Super Sonic 2 state, he can unleash the full power of the cyber corruption at the risk of never being able to upgrade to this form again. Cyber Sonic has similar feats to Super Sonic 2 with the only differences being that he now has a greater power for all of his abilites. The once golden color of his fur is now a digital color of black and blue covering everything, his eyes now a grey color, and now is glitching out because of the cyber corruption. Similar to Super Sonic, this transformation requires rings to maintain so once out of rings Sonic returns to base form.

    Hyper Sonic

    • Hyper Sonic is a transformation that exceeds the power of Super Sonic. In order for Sonic to transform into the Hyper form, Sonic needs to gather all seven of the Super Emeralds. The Super Emeralds are an upgraded form of the Chaos Emeralds as they now intuned with the Master Emerald, which controls the power of the Chaos Emeralds, anyways once gathered, Sonic can go Hyper and have feats that far surpass any form mention prior. He has boosted Super Sonic strength, Super Sonic speed, Super Sonic flight, Super Sonic chaos abilites, and overall is one of the strongest forms now mentioned. Sonic is in this multicolor form that changes from one to another within a microsecond, his eyes now a green color, and is surrounded in this aura of white sparks.

    Ultra Sonic

    • Ultra Sonic is a transformation only mention in the Archie Comics however just because it is in a different universe doesn't mean it does rival some of the other transformations as this transformation rivals the power of Hyper Sonic. In order for Sonic to transform into Ultra Sonic, he must absorb an abundance of Power Rings which are similar to rings but grant a boost of power to the user. Sonic has the same feats of Hyper Sonic however the power is boosted, so there is boosted Hyper Sonic speed, strength, flight, chaos abilites, and this transformation is the strongest that Sonic has. Sonic may look like he is in base form however, he has rings going around him and this aura of black stars surrounding him.
